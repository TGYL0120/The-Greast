Mark Elliot Zuckerberg

Bornin the United States in 1984, Zuckerberg grew up with his father,
a dentist, and his mother, a psychiatrist. He also developed a dental office program
run by his father when he was 11 years old, and produced music-play program Synapses
while attending high school and received offers for acquisition and employment from Microsoft
and AOL, but turned down the offer and entered Harvard University in September 2002. In 2004,
he created Facebook, a service for Harvard University students to share contacts and manage
their connections with friends, and expanded the list of applicants to universities across the U.S.,
including Stanford and Columbia. Since then, anyone from around the world has been allowed to
join Facebook, with the number of Facebook subscribers reaching 2.3 billion as of 2019. -wikipedia-
